EPC has the service team and resources to support your ongoing backup power needs. From installation to maintenance to technical support, we are here to ensure your systems are operating effectively and reliably.

Battery Disposal
All cradle-to-grave EPA certificates provided.
Battery Maintenance
Annual and quarterly maintenance provided as per manufacturer specs. Remedial clean-up also provided on VLA and VRLA systems.

Temp and Voltage modules for local or remote monitoring to include integration of batteries, disconnect, and power systems.

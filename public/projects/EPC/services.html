Battery Recycling & Disposal

Ease your recycling and disposal worries with EPC's Telcordia-trained technicians. Our technicians are certified to dispose of hazmat batteries and any related materials. EPC places the utmost importance in safe and responsible recycling and disposal practices. We work with local facilities in order to comply with Environmental Protection Agency rules and regulations. EPC issues certified documentation in order to provide sufficient evidence that the consumption and disposal of lead-acid batteries has aligned with EPA standards.

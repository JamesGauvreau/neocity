PowerSafe G Battery

PowerSafe G batteries offer field-tested performance that meets industry requirements for long life and less maintenance. Copper inserts in the posts maintain excellent short duration high-current performance, while individual posts enable individual cell monitoring. PowerSafe G batteries also feature the Slide-Lock™ post seal that allows for natural plate growth over time without damaging the seal.

Features & Benefits

  • Capacity range: 930 to 3900 Ah
  • 20-year life expectancy in float service at 77°F (25°C)
  • Electrolyte reserve reduces watering requirements
  • 100% initial capacity out of the box

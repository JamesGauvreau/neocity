PowerSafe F Battery

Ideal for central office, cellular and microwave applications, PowerSafe F batteries all feature a multi-directional post design that allows cells to be configured in any orientation – either perpendicular to the rack support rails to optimize footprint, or parallel to enable easy maintenance while the system is online. PowerSafe F batteries also feature the EnerSys exclusive Slide-Lock™ post seal that allows for natural plate growth over time without degrading the seal, plus an innovative tongue and groove jar-to-cover seal to prevent leaks.

Features & Benefits

  • Capacity range: 840 to 1810 Ah
  • Individual posts to monitor individual cell performance
  • Flame retardant container and cover - UL94-VO

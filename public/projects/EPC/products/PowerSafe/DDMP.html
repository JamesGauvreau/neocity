PowerSafe DDMP Battery

PowerSafe DDmP batteries combine the performance of an enhanced cell design with the flexibility of a superior racking system. The cells incorporate a long-life grid alloy, thicker plates, and a catalyst for longer life, and are encased in a dedicated protective steel cans that are easy to install and maintain constant, uniform compression for the life of the cell.

In addition, the easy to assemble racking system provides total flexibility for system configuration and allows fast, simple installation in the most challenging locations.â€‹

Features & Benefits

  • 20-year design life on standby float applications
  • 100% initial battery capacity with up to 2000 Ah / 4094 wpc in a single cell
  • Rack frame design allows for maximum heat dissipation
  • Configurations: 2, 3, 4 or 6 cells wide in single cell modules; 2, 4 or 6 in 2-cell modules
  • Available for any footprint specifications for telecom, datacenter, industrial and industrial switchgear applications

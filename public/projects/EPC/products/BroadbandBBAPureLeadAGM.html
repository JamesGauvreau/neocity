Broadband BBA Pure Lead AGM Battery

C&D Broadband AGM (BBA) batteries deliver world-class power for mission-critical applications. The rugged case design with ribbed jar walls is key to maintaining battery performance, sustains cell compression, and provides cooling channels. Proprietary Pure Lead Plus technology and high-density paste increases service life through increased temperatures.

Features & Benefits

  • 12-volt monobloc
  • Technology: Valve Regulated Lead Acid (VRLA) Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM)
  • The BBA uses MSE Pure Lead Plus Technology for ultra-low float current and extended battery life up to a 7+ year service life in uncontrolled environments
  • MSE Pure lead paste using ultra-high purity 99.99% pure lead oxide
  • Up to 255 runtime minutes
  • Proprietary high-density paste reduces float current
  • Increases battery life and reduces the risk of thermal run-away
  • 20% better float current than standard AGM
  • Efficient gas recombination decreases gassing/water loss
  • Operating temperature range: Discharge: -40˚F to 160˚F, Charge: -10˚F to 140˚F (with temperature compensation)
  • Centered lug for improved performance
  • Radial grid design for reduced resistance and enhanced moderate to high-rate performance
  • Advanced lead alloys designed to minimize positive grid corrosion and increase service life
  • Flame-arresting one-way pressure-relief vent for safety and long life
  • Can be used in any orientation. Upright, side, or end mounting recommended
  • Not restricted for surface transport: classified as non-hazardous material as related to DOT-CFR Title 49 parts 171-189.

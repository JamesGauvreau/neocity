High Rate Max UPS-MR AGM Battery

C&D Technologies’ High Rate Max (MR) batteries provide the constant power backup that Uninterruptible Power Supply systems require. The high energy density and quality designs assure that your business systems run uninterrupted.

Features & Benefits

  • The UL V-2 Polypropylene case is extremely durable and reduces water loss, especially in batteries which are in service for 5 years or more
  • Flame retardant polypropylene case and cover compliant with UL94-V2
  • Patented Long Life Alloy has the lowest calcium levels in the industry – minimizing grid growth, reducing gassing, and extending battery life
  • Patented UL Recognized Flame-arresting vents in each cell for safety and long life
  • Designed with the same recombination thermal runaway prevention, gassing and flame-retardant characteristics of the Bellcore 4228 compliant Telecom products
  • Thermally welded case-to-cover bond to eliminate leakage
  • Can be operated in any orientation – upright, side, or end mounting recommended
  • Terminal versatility – ease of diagnostic readings with C&D Ohmic Ring®
  • Reduced headspace driving higher energy density in cabinet or rack applications
  • True Front Access available: innovative front terminal design maximizing energy density with direct connect extrusion fusion weld technology
  • Larger grid lug – 25% wider
  • Computer generated optimized radial grid design for the most efficient power channeling out of the grid
  • Thicker wires near post to maximize power transfer from active material during high-rate discharges
  • Optimized grid alloy for extended life in UPS applications
  • Thin plate design maximizes surface area for higher power density
  • UL-recognized component
  • No restrictions for air/surface/water transport - Classified as non-hazardous material as related to DOT-CFR Title 49 parts 171-189 - Complies with IATA/ICAO Special Provisions A67 - Classified non-hazardous material per IMDG Amendment 27

TEL Long Duration Series AGM Battery

C&D Technologies TEL Long Duration series batteries are designed to provide standby power to critical applications in the Telecom industry. The batteries in this long duration series are Telcordia-tested, qualified to meet superior standards on extensive life cycles, and ensure the longest and most reliable service life possible.

Features & Benefits

  • Telcordia certified life exceeding 10 years
  • Telcordia tested and qualified to meet all SR-4228 requirements
  • Flame-arresting, one-way pressure-relief vent for safety and long life
  • Thermally welded case-to-cover bond ensures a leak-proof seal
  • Flame-retardant polypropylene case and cover compliant with UL94 V-0
  • Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) technology for efficient gas recombination 99% plus
  • Eurobat Classification: Long Life
  • Multicell design for ease of installation and maintenance
  • Not restricted for transport:
    • Air transport - Classified as non-hazardous material, complies with IATA/ICAO Special Provision A67
    • Surface transport - Classified as non-hazardous material, complies with DOT-CFR Title 49 parts 171-189
    • Water transport - Classified as non-hazardous material, complies with IMDG exception 238

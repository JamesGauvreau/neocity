ATevo Series Stationary Float Battery Charger

HindlePower's ATevo continues the AT series legacy of being the industry's most trusted operational stationary float battery charger. Utilizing proven SCR technology, ATevo is designed and manufactured with the same superior quality and reliability you've come to expect from AT series chargers. Equipped with the same AT Series features, the ATevo provides a simple user experience allowing for a "set it and forget it" approach to battery charging. New features and expandability will enable the utility to meet today's evolving operational and reliability issues.

Features & Benefits

  • Same legacy control as AT10/AT30 product
  • Standard features including: Digital Ground Fault Alarm/Metering, AC Ripple Alarm, End of Discharge Alarm, Rectifier Over Temperature Alarm, and More
  • Fully Graphical LCD
  • Digital Metering and Event Logging
  • Quick and Easy Field Upgradable Firmware
  • 3 Levels of Password Protection
  • Easy Expandable Platform
  • Downloadable Manual via QR Code
  • Multiple Communications Platforms such as DNP, MODBUS, IEC61850

Product Quick Facts

  • AC Input Voltage: 120, 208, 240, 480 | | 220, 380/416 @ 50-60Hz
  • AC Input Voltage Tolerance: +10%, -12%
  • AC Input Frequency Tolerance: ±5%
  • AC Input Efficiency: 85-90% typical for 130Vdc at 50-100% load
  • DC Output Voltage Ratings: 24, 48, 130 or 260* Vdc nominal
  • DC Output Continuous Rating: 110% rated current at maximum equalize voltage at -10 to +50ºC
  • DC Output Transient Rating: Per IEEE 2405
  • DC Output Current Limit Adjustment Range: 50% to 110% rated output
  • DC Output Voltage Regulation: ±0.25% for line, load and temp. variations (*Regulation at max. extended equalize voltages may not meet ±0.25%)
  • Electrical Noise: 32dBrnc
  • DC Output Ripple: 24/48Vdc: Filtered on battery 30mVrms | Filtered off battery 1% Vrms | Battery Eliminator 30mVrms
  • DC Output Ripple: 130Vdc: Filtered on battery 100mVrms |Filtered off battery 2% Vrms | Battery Eliminator 100mVrms | Super Eliminator 30mVrms
  • DC Output Ripple: 260Vdc: Filtered on battery 200mVrms | Filtered off battery 2% Vrms | Battery Eliminator 200mVrms
  • DC Output Surge Withstand Capability: Designed to meet IEEE-472, ANSI C37.90a
  • 3rd Party Approvals: IEEE 2405 | CSA | FCC
  • Seismic Certification: IEEE 693/IBC CBC
  • Operating Ambient Temperature: -1°F to 122°F (-18°C to 50°C) w/o derating
  • Operating Altitude: 3,300 feet (1,000 meters) above sea level without derating
  • Relative Humidity: 0% to 95% (without condensation)
  • Audible Noise: Less than 65 dBA at any point 5ft (1.5m) from any vertical surface of enclosure
  • Monitoring: HindleHealth® System

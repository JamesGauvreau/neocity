LCZ 2500 Flooded Battery

The LCZ 2500 battery provides unparalleled reliability and performance over the entire life of the battery. Proprietary manufacturing techniques paired with high-quality components ensure a long battery life with reduced maintenance.

Features & Benefits

  • Long-life positive plates cast with a proprietary process and alloy
  • Waterbath charging results in consistently formed plates for reliable performance out of the box (no cycling required in the field)
  • Proprietary expander enables stable and uniform float voltages, reducing the need to equalize and resulting in proven 20-year service life
  • A suspended positive plate permits free growth without pressure on the jar and cover
  • Flame-retardant covers enhance battery plant safety with self-extinguishing properties LOI >32%, UL 94V-0
  • Low-evaporation, flame-arrester vent to extend watering intervals and prevent external sparks from reacting with the hydrogen inside the cell
  • A transparent container allows visual inspection of plates
  • Soft rubber post seal minimizes stress on the post reducing maintenance requirements
  • Computer-controlled heli-arc welded post seal results in a consistent and reliable seal for less maintenance and longer product life
  • No requirement for periodic specific gravity readings
  • Narrow 17” (43.18 cm) depth racks available – NEBS approved
  • NEBS-approved battery systems (cells and racks) that meet telecom companies’ specifications

KCT Flooded Battery

The NEBS approved KCT battery offers a wide range of capacities within a small footprint, making it ideal for a variety of Telecom applications. C&D proprietary technology can be found throughout the design to ensure a reliable and long battery life.

Features & Benefits

  • Single-cell design for reduced weight per jar and ease of handling
  • Proven history of 20-year service life
  • Dual hole post design allows connector maintenance without removing the battery from service (KCR-17 through KCR-21)
  • Low-evaporation, flame-arrester vent to extend watering intervals and prevent external sparks from reacting with the hydrogen inside the cell
  • 100% of cells are capacity tested in the factory
  • Waterbath charging results in consistently formed plates for reliable performance
  • Computer-controlled heli-arc welded post seals result in consistent and reliable seals for less maintenance and longer product life.

LCT-HP Flooded Battery

The NEBS approved LCT-High Performance (HP) battery excels in Telecom applications and are engineered to provide superior performance. The LCT-1805HP offers 1805 Ampere-hours in a traditional LCT-1680 jar size, packing more power in the same footprint. C&D's proprietary technology in the LCT-HP battery ensures a reliable and long battery life.

Features & Benefits

  • Electrical testing to 95% capacity on every cell prior to shipping assures performance of every battery upon delivery
  • Long life positive plates cast with a proprietary process and alloy
  • Waterbath charging results inconsistently formed plates for reliable performance out of the box (no cycling required in the field)
  • Suspended positive plate permits free growth without pressure on jar and cover
  • Flame-retardant covers & jars enhance battery plant safety with self-extinguishing properties LOI >32%, UL 94V-0
  • Low-evaporation, flame-arrester vent to extend watering intervals and prevent external sparks from reacting with the hydrogen inside the cell
  • LCT-1805HP offers 1805Ah in a traditional LCT-1680 jar size
  • Transparent container allows visual inspection of plates
  • Soft rubber post seal minimizes stress on post reducing maintenance requirements
  • Computer controlled heli-arc welded post seal results in consistent and reliable seal for less maintenance and longer product life
  • All posts and connectors reside above the jar cover
  • Optional terminal and connector cover available (P/N RD00907-“L”)
  • Narrow 17” (43.18 cm) depth racks available – NEBS qualified
  • NEBS approved battery systems (cells and racks) that meet telecom companies’ specifications

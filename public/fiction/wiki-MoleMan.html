Harvey Elder alias Mole Man v. Judith Barzilay (2nd Cir. 2005) is a U.S. Second Circuit Court of Appeals defamation case brought by Elder for a comment in Barzilay’s ruling of Toy Biz, Inc. v. United States, namely that Elder was not a human being.[1]

The suit was dismissed on the grounds that Elder (1) previously forfeited U.S. citizenship in favor of the kingdom of Subterranea[a] and (2) had attempted on multiple occasions to conquer or devastate the surface world (including the United States) with earthquake machines and other devices.

Tooth-growing Spell

A.K.A. Tooth-growing Hex. 

Incantation: Densaugeo. Color: Violet. Taught in: 5th year DADA (1988-89).

Effect: Causes the target's tooth or teeth to regrow when damaged or missing. 

Source(s): Witchcraft & Wizardry.

Tooth-shrinking Spell

Incantation: Densarto.

Effect: Reduces the size of a tooth or multiple teeth.

Source(s): Witchcraft & Wizardry.

Tooth-straightening Spell

Incantation: Dencorrigo. 

Effect: Straightens the target’s teeth by means of transfiguration.

Miscasting: Performed improperly, the teeth may be transfigured into something else entirely.

Source(s): Canon, with an original incantation and some speculation as to the method.

Toppling Charm

Incantation: Occumbo.

Effect: Knocks over the target.

Miscasting: The target may only budge a couple inches. 

Other information: The target must typically be no bigger than a fist for this spell to work properly.

Source(s): Hogwarts is Here. 

Tracking Charm

Incantation: Appare Vestigium. Light: Gold, as swirling "dust."

Effect: Reveals and illuminates traces of recent magical activity, including in the form of footprints.

Other information: It is not difficult to thwart or even deceive this spell, which makes it only somewhat useful to the DMLE.

Source(s): Canon, with some elaboration.

Transformation Repair Spell

Incantation: Reparifarge. Taught in: 2nd year Transfiguration (1986-87).

Effect: Reverses the effects of a botched transfiguration. Usually.

Source(s): Canon, with a name from Witchcraft & Wizardry.

Transmogrifian Torture Curse

Incantation: Unknown. 

Effect: Slowly, painfully, and ultimately fatally transfigures the target.

Source(s): Canon.

Tree-moving Charm

Incantation: Mobiliarbus. Taught in: 3rd year Herbology. 

Effect: Uproots and levitates a tree (or other plant) safely to a new location, which must already be dug out.

Other information: Standard levitation charms are usually up to the job, but some plants are physically or magically delicate and require more sensitive handling. 

Source(s): Witchcraft & Wizardry, with some elaboration. 

Trip Jinx

Incantation: Claudeo.

Effect: Causes the target to trip and fall down. 

Other information: Has no effect on targets that are standing still.

Source(s): Witchcraft & Wizardry.

Tunneling Charm

Incantation: Efosso.

Effect: Digs out a pit, which lengthens as the spell is prolonged.

Other information: Useful for herbologists working with long- or strong-rooted plants, and also used for laying irrigation systems. Can cause structural damage to the building. More durable material causes a noticeable feeling of "resistance" while the spell is working. 

Source(s): Hogwarts is Here, with some elaboration. 

Twitchy-ears Hex

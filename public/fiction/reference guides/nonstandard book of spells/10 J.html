Jelly-brain Jinx

Incantation: Nervosa Wibbly. 

Effect: Impairs certain parts of the brain, making it difficult to concentrate.

Source(s): Canon, with original incantation (derived from the locomotor wibbly incantation of the Jelly-Legs Curse) and some speculation regarding the mechanism.

Jelly-fingers Curse

Incantation: Articulus Wibbly. 

Effect: Impairs “nervous functions” in the target’s fingers, making it difficult to hold and manipulate objects.

Source(s): Canon, with original incantation (derived from the locomotor wibbly incantation of the Jelly-Legs Curse) and some speculation regarding the mechanism.

Jelly-legs Curse

Incantation: Locomotor Wibbly. Light: Orange. Taught in: 3rd year DADA. 

Effect: Impairs “nervous functions” in the target’s legs, which, given the role of legs in a person’s life, generally means that those legs will collapse from under them.

Source(s): Canon, with some speculation regarding the mechanism.

Reawakening Charm

Incantation: Reviviescito.

Effect: Gently nudges the target's mind and body to awaken them. 

Source(s): Hogwarts is Here.

Rebounding Curse

Incantation: Circa Repercutit. Light: Yellow.

Effect: Inflicts terrible pain on the target.

Other information: Named for the fact that the curse will bounce off objects until it hits a living creature. In fact, the curse has no effect until it rebounds for the first time. It is a useful spell when fighting someone who is unfamiliar with it or just isn't paying attention.  

Source(s): Original.  

Recitation Spell

Incantation: Revolve.

Effect: Causes the target object to "speak." 

Variability: The target object's voice, and the words which it recites, are determined beforehand at the time of casting. 

Other information: Poor concentration can impregnate the spell with the wrong words, wrong tone, etc. 

Source(s): Hogwarts is Here.

Rectification Transfiguration

Incantation: Reparifarge. Light: White. Taught in: 2nd Year Transfiguration (1985-86).

Effect: Reverses an incomplete transfiguration (most of the time). 

Source(s): Canon.

Reductor Curse

Incantation: Reducto. Light: Blue. Taught in: 5th Year DADA (1988-89); unclear whether a typical DADA professor who wasn't Umbridge would have taught the Reductor Curse in 1995. Learnable by 4th Year students.  

Effect: Blasts the target to pieces, sometimes a fine mist or pile of ashes.

Other information: More difficult to use against living targets. 

Source(s): Canon, with some elaboration from Witchcraft & Wizardry and myself.

Referencing Spell

Incantation: Perscriptiogripha. 

Effect: Magically writes a bibliography upon the enchanted parchment. 

Variability: Ink is usually used for this spell but the caster can make do with other materials or even (with care) scorch the bibliography into the parchment. 

Source(s): Hogwarts is Here.

Refilling Charm

Incantation: Repleo. Taught in: Unknown, but it is implied (HBP ch. 12) that 6th Year Harry had performed it previously in verbal form. 

Effect: Refills the target container with a specific substance by displacing it from another location.

Miscasting: When performed unskillfully, the spell may result in a noise not unlike the “popping” of apparition.

Source(s): Canon, with an incantation from Witchcraft & Wizardry and some elaboration from myself.

Repelling Spell

Incantation: Recedo.

Effect: Repels a moving object or creature, harmless sending it back from whence it came. 

Other information: The spell can only repel small targets, e.g. cats, knives, quaffles. 

Source(s): Witchcraft & Wizardry.

Revealing Charm (General)

Incantation: Revelio. Light: Blue. Taught in: Unknown. Difficult but not impossible for a 2nd Year student to perform. 

Effect: Reveals concealed messages, passages, and other objects.

Variability: There are many common variations which are intended to reveal the presence or nature of other things: Homenum Revelio for humans, Specialis Revelio for lingering enchantments, etc. 

Source(s): Canon, with some elaboration. 

Revealing Charm (Text)

Incantation: Aparecium. Taught in: Unknown, but 2nd Year Hermione Granger could cast the spell. 

Effect: Reveals hidden writing in the target manuscript.

Other information: Distinct from, and more specialized than, the more common Revealing Charm.  

Source(s): Canon.

Reviving Charm

Incantation: Rennervate. Light: Red, as a brilliant flash. Taught in: 4th year Charms. 

Effect: Awakens a sleeping or stunned target.

Other information: Can counteract a single Stunning Charm, but not several. Ineffectual against Dark magic. It may take time for the target to awaken, and the target may be confused or groggy. Depending on the spell being counteracted, the target may struggle to remember events immediately receding unconsciousness or even temporarily struggle to form short-term memories. 

Source(s): Canon, with elaboration from Hogwarts is Here and Witchcraft & Wizardry.

Revulsion Jinx

Incantation: Relashio. Light: Purple, as sparks. Taught in: Unknown, but performed by 4th Year Harry Potter. 

Effect: Compels the target to release its hold on something, whether the hold is maintained by fingers, knots, or another method. 

Variability: With precision, it is possible to push the target back by several steps or knock them to the ground. Underwater, the spell-light leaves a trail of boiling water in its wake. 

Other information: Living beings can attempt to resist this spell.

Source(s): Canon, with elaboration from Hogwarts is Here and Witchcraft & Wizardry.

Ripping Charm

Incantation: Guidoleti. 

Effect: Inflicts grievous harm on a living target by tearing flesh and bone. 

Other notes: The damage inflicted by this spell is resistant (though not completely unaffected) by magical forms of healing. For this reason, and because it can only target a living being, it is classified as Dark magic in Britain. 

Source(s): Original. 

Rope-Binding Jinx

Incantation: Incarcerous. 

Effect: Conjures cords or ropes from the caster's wand, which wrap around the target.

Variability: The bindings may restrict only part of the target's body, gag their mouth, constrict more tightly over time, or actively resist attempts to untie or cut them. 

Source(s): Canon. 

Rotation Charm

Incantation: Circumrota. 

Effect: Turns or rotates the object in place.

Source(s): Canon.

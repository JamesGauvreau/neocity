Obliteration Charm

Incantation: Unknown. 

Effect: Removes footprints (e.g. in snow).

Variability: May also be used to remove other traces of a person’s presence, like fingernails or recently-made writing.

Source(s): Canon, with some elaboration.

Obscuring Charm

Incantation: Obscuro. Wand movement: Unknown. Light: None.

Conjures a blindfold around the target's eyes.

The blindfold is magically held in place; physical, non-magical attempts to remove the blindfold will fail.

Source(s): Canon, with some elaboration from Witchcraft & Wizardry.

Onslaught Jinx

A.K.A. Siccing Spell

Incantation: Oppugno. 

Effect: Directs moveable object(s) or conjured creature(s) to attack the victim, under the control of the caster. 

Variability: Can be cast on a single object or on a stack, pile, or container of objects of the same type. Greater precision permits looseness around "same type." 

Source(s): Canon, with an alternate name and elaboration from Witchcraft & Wizardry.

Orrery Charm

Incantation: Alasturlab. 

Effect: Light flows from the end of the caster's wand to form planets, stars, and so forth in miniature imitation of the night sky, centered on the caster.

Other information: Originally called the Planetarium Charm.

Source(s): Original.

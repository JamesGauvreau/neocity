Key-word Search Charm

Incantation: Buscalabra [X], where X is the word being searched for. 

Effect: Opens the target book to the first (or next, on successive incantations) mention of that word in the text. 

Variability: Greater precision can identify synonyms, variant spellings, etc. 

Other information: Harder to effectively perform with a greater amount of pages, or with unbound pages. 

Source(s): Hogwarts is Here.

Killing Curse

Incantation: Avada Kedavra. Light: Green.

Effect: Kills the target, no save vs Death, do not pass Go, do not collect £200.

Source(s): Canon.

Knee-reversing Hex

Incantation: Retrogeno. 

Effect: Reverses the target’s knees, so that they face and bend backward.

Miscasting: Performed improperly, the transfiguration will just end up snapping the knees (which, in a duel, may be just what you were hoping for).

Source(s): Canon, with some speculation and an incantation from Witchcraft & Wizardry.

Knit-up Charm

A.K.A. Body-Mending Charm, Mending Charm (see also Mending Charm), Patch-Up Charm

Incantation: Episkey, from the Greek επισκευή (episkevi), "repair." 

Effect: Heals relatively minor injuries, like broken noses, split lips, and toes.

Variability: Proficient casters may also relieve pain with this spell. 

Other information: This may briefly cause a sudden temperature change (first hot, then cold). 

Source(s): Canon, with some elaboration on the names, and an alternate name from Witchcraft & Wizardry.

Knitting Charm

Incantation: Conferbo. 

Effect: Enchants a set of knitting needles to knit autonomously. 

Source(s): Witchcraft & Wizardry.

Knock-back Jinx

Incantation: Flipendo. Light: Blue. Other signs: Loud bang. 

Effect: Knocks the target backwards. 

Other information: The impact feels like a blow to the chest or the head. The latter may leave the target disoriented. Can break fragile objects.

Source(s): Canon.

Knocking Spell

Incantation: Ominous Vselezhazatus. 

Effect: Forcefully opens a door.  

Source(s): Tanya Grotter.

Knot-tying Charm

Incantation: Nodatis. 

Effect: Enchants a rope, thread, or similar object to tie itself into a knot.  

Source(s): Hogwarts is Here.

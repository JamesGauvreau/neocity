Launching Charm

Incantation: Alarte Ascendare. Light: Red. Taught in: Unclear, but performable by Hermione Granger, post-6th Year.

Effect: Launches the target into the air in a sudden, powerful burst. Performed correctly, and absent other factors (e.g. interference from other spells), the target will land unharmed.

Other information: Even Gilderoy Lockhart was capable of performing (something like) this spell, for whatever that's worth.

Source(s): Canon, with a name and some elaboration from Witchcraft & Wizardry.

Leek Jinx

Incantation: Aliumauro. Light: Yellow. 

Effect: Conjures leeks from the target’s ears. Rather painful, and capable of causing damage to the ears.

Source(s): Canon, with some speculation and an incantation from Witchcraft & Wizardry.

Leg-growing Spell

Incantation: Ambulafors. 

Effect: The target inanimate object grows functional legs.

Other information: Unattended objects may wander. 

Source(s): Witchcraft & Wizardry.

Leg-locker Curse

Incantation: Locomotor Mortis. Light: Purple. Taught in: 5th Year Charms (but see below).

Effect: Binds the target’s legs together.

Other information: Found in the spellbook Curses and Counter-Curses by Vindictus Viridian. Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley learned it (to unknown success) in 1st Year. Draco Malfoy successfully used it in 1st Year.

Source(s): Canon.

Leg-unlocking Spell

Incantation: Locomotor Liberari. 

Effect: Releases the target from the effects of the Leg-locker Curse.

Source(s): Hogwarts is Here.

Levitation Charm

Incantation: Wingardium Levioso. Wand movement: SWISH AND FLICK. Light: None. Taught in: 1st Year.

Effect: Causes the target to levitate.

Other information: It is impossible, whether by this spell or any other, to levitate oneself, and this charm in particular does not work on humans. Also a term for the category of spells to which the Levitation Charm belongs.

Source(s): Canon, with some elaboration.

Lock-breaking Charm

Incantation: Portaberto.

Effect: Shatters the target lock (potentially beyond repair) or rips it from the door. 

Source(s): Witchcraft & Wizardry.

Locking Charm

Incantation: Colloportus. Light: None. 

Effect: Shuts and locks the target door, or wedges the target door stuck if it has no lock.

Variability: With skill and an understanding of the charmwork, it is possible to confer some resistance against unlocking spells, but this is an inefficient method by which to do that. It is possible to use this spell to close a door as well. 

Other information: The lock's proper key can still unlock it, but the lock is otherwise impermeable to non-destructive Muggle methods of bypassing a lock. Found in The Standard Book of Spells, Grade 1.

Source(s): Canon, with some elaboration from myself and Hogwarts is Here.

Locomotion Charm

Incantation: Locomotor [X]. 

Effect: Levitates the target a few inches or feet above the ground, then moves it through the air according to the caster’s direction.

Other information: Cannot be used against a living target.

Source(s): Canon, with some elaboration from Witchcraft & Wizardry.

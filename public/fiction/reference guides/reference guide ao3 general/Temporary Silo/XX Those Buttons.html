Dashboard

Profile

Things that I recommend adding to your profile:

  • Blanket statement giving permission for derivative works based on your own fan works.
  • Some information about yourself and how you fit into fandom: how did you get into this, what have you done here, and maybe some other stuff. Betts has written about the importance of fan author biographies and has suggestions for other things to include if you're willing to get more detailed. As a historian of Harry Potter fandom, I can't stress enough how plausible it is that somebody might track down your fic and have some questions about its author, twenty years after you wrote it.

How this guide uses the term "canon," resources for finding canonical information, and a few deep dive analyses of canon that others have written.

Defining Canon

Practically every reader takes the seven Harry Potter-titled books, PS to DH, as canon. Most will also take the books Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them and Quidditch Through the Ages as canon.
